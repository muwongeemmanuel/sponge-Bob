Admission

Prospective parents are welcome to visit our program most weekdays by visiting our office between 9:15 to 11:15. Please call for availability. We are happy to share our program information, show you our classrooms, and answer all your questions. You are invited to bring your child with you during this initial visit as long as he or she is in good health.

Classes are filled from our wait list.

If you wish, a wait form will be provided to you during your visit. Wait lists are kept in order of date filed and prospective parents are called when a space opens which corresponds to their wishes for program, age group, days of the week desired. Preference is given to siblings of children currently enrolled in our program. Once called from our wait list, placement is secured by providing a non-refundable annual registration fee and a deposit (refundable with a one-month notice) to hold the space for your child. This deposit is not your child’s first-month tuition. It is a deposit which stays on file in your child’s account from which outstanding charges will be deducted and the remainder will be fully refunded when you are finished with our program.

Required state forms must be completed before your child may be left in our care. For Toddler and Nursery School classes, we require at least two visits when your child can play in your presence and become acquainted with new friends and the classroom or playground before his or her first day. For Infant Program enrollment, a child’s first stay with us is limited to one hour and is gradually built up depending upon your child’s own comfort level.

Each member of our staff is trained and ready to work with your child and provide reassurance until your return. We want “school” to be very positive experience for each child. The bond we build with your child from the first day in our center is aimed at providing security and trust that will carry through each school day.
